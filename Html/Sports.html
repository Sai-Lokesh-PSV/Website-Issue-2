IPL

IPL was established in 2008 and currently consists of eight teams in eight cities across India. The inaugural IPL season was won by Rajasthan Royals. Mumbai Indians have the most IPL trophies at the moment. It is played in the home-away system.

THE ASHES

The Ashes is a Test cricket series played between England and Australia. The Ashes are regarded as being held by the team that most recently won the series. If the series is drawn, the team that currently holds the Ashes retains the trophy.

Cricket during COVID

Due to COVID, all sports leagues were cancelled or postponed including the cricket leagues. Last year, the Indian Premier League (IPL) was supposed to start on 29th March, 2020 but as the situation of the pandemic worsened, it started on 19th September, 2020. The matches are played with utmost caution. The players are tested beforehand and are quarantined in bubble rooms, which are used for social distancing. To avoid exposure, fans were not allowed to come and spectate the match in the stadium. This year IPL is being played across six cities: Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Chennai, Delhi, Mumbai and Kolkata.
