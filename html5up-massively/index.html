What is the
Music Therapy

Music therapy is the use of organized sounds and music within an evolving relationship between a client and therapist to support and encourage physical, mental, social and emotional wellbeing.

Thgerapuetic device?

Physiological and Physical reasons- music sprouts creative and constructive feelings and thoughts. 70% of the human body comprises water. Sound travels faster than water and air, stimulating the cells in our body, due to which the immunity of our body improves. It balances the pulse rate, breathing and blood circulation evolves. Several researchers have shown that music enhances the functions of neural networks, slow the heart rate, lower blood pressure, reduce the levels of stress hormones and inflammatory cytokines. ... Psychological reasons- Music balances the left and right parts of the brain, bringing an equilibrium between an individual's feelings, thoughts, logical, analytical and scientific reasoning.
